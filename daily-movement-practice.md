# Daily Movement Practice

**Every day. No exceptions.**

---

## Mobility — Spine (8 min)

| Exercise            | Target   | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| ------------------- | -------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cat-cow             | 10 reps  | On hands and knees. Round spine up (cat), drop belly and lift chest (cow). One vertebra at a time, not hinging at one spot.                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| Open book rotations | 8/side   | Lie on your side, knees stacked and bent to 90°. Bottom arm extended in front of you. Open your top arm up and over, rotating your upper back, and try to lay it flat on the floor on the other side. Follow your hand with your eyes. Breathe out as you open. Keep your knees stacked — don't let the top knee lift. |
| Prone cobra hold    | 2 × 30s  | Face down, lift chest, arms out to sides, thumbs up. Squeeze between shoulder blades.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| Seated spinal twist | 30s/side | One leg extended, cross other foot over. Twist toward bent knee using opposite elbow for leverage.                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| Bridge              | 3 × 20s  | Start with glute bridge. Progress toward full back bridge or wall walkdowns over weeks.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |

## Mobility — Shoulders (8 min)

| Exercise                           | Target   | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| ---------------------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Shoulder CARs                      | 5/arm    | Biggest, slowest arm circle possible. Note where you hit restriction.                                                                                                                                                                            |
| Prone shoulder ER stretch          | 30s/side | Face down, arm out at shoulder height, elbow 90°. Let forearm hang, then rotate toward ceiling into external rotation. Hold end range.                                                                                                           |
| Overhead lat stretch               | 40s/side | Grab doorframe overhead, step away, sink hips to the side. Feel it from armpit down the torso.                                                                                                                                                   |
| Doorframe pec stretch (high angle) | 40s/side | Forearm on doorframe at 120–135°, step through. Targets pec minor.                                                                                                                                                                               |
| Wall slides                        | 10 reps  | Back flat against wall, goalpost arms, slide up. Stop where contact breaks. Push end range over weeks.                                                                                                                                           |
| Sleeper stretch                    | 30s/side | Lie on your throwing side. Shoulder straight out in front at 90°, elbow bent 90° so forearm points at ceiling. Gently push hand toward the floor with your other hand. Stretches internal rotation — the range that tightens most from throwing. |

## Mobility — Hips & Ankles (9 min)

| Exercise                                             | Target   | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| ---------------------------------------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Prone hip internal rotation                          | 30s/side | Lie face down, bend one knee to 90° so your foot points at the ceiling. Keeping the knee bent, try to pull your ankle toward the ceiling by rotating the thigh inward. You'll feel this deep in the hip. Don't force it — work at your end range and let it open up over weeks. |
| Groin foam rolling                                   | 60s/side | Lie face down, bring one knee out to the side with the inner thigh on the foam roller. Roll slowly from near the knee up toward the groin. Spend extra time on tender spots. Focus on the right side.                                                                           |
| 90/90 hold with forward lean                         | 30s/side | Sit with both knees at 90°, lean chest over front shin. Deep hip ER stretch.                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| Cossack squat                                        | 6/side   | Wide stance, sink into deep side lunge, other leg straight. Heel stays down. 3s down, 3s up.                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| Half-kneeling hip flexor stretch with overhead reach | 40s/side | Back knee down, squeeze glute, lean forward. Reach same-side arm overhead and lean away. Opens hip flexor, oblique, and lat.                                                                                                                                                    |
| Deep squat hold                                      | 60s      | Feet wider than shoulders, toes out, sink deep. Elbows push knees out. Heels on floor.                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| Wall ankle dorsiflexion                              | 10/side  | Face wall, foot ~10cm away. Drive knee over toes to touch wall, heel stays down. Move foot further back as range improves.                                                                                                                                                      |

## Movement — Balance & Body Control (4 min)

| Exercise                      | Target   | Description                                                                                                  |
| ----------------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Single-leg stand, eyes closed | 45s/side | Stand tall, one foot up, close eyes. Reset if you wobble.                                                    |
| Single-leg hip hinge          | 8/side   | Hinge forward on one leg, free leg extends behind. Touch floor, hold bottom 2s, return. Slow and controlled. |

---

## Movement — Core & Rotation (5 min)

| Exercise                   | Target | Description                                                                                                                                                 |
| -------------------------- | ------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Dead bug                   | 8/side | On back, arms toward ceiling, knees 90° over hips. Lower opposite arm and leg simultaneously. Lower back stays pressed to floor. Breathe out as you extend. |
| Rotational ground-up twist | 8/side | Wide split stance. Rotate trunk explosively from back hip toward front leg, pivoting back foot. Focus on sequencing: feet → hips → trunk → arms. No weight. |

---

## Movement — Inversions (4 min)

| Exercise          | Target     | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| ----------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Pike hold         | 3 × 20–30s | Hands on floor, feet on a chair or bench, walk your hands back so your hips are as high as possible and your torso is as vertical as you can get. Hold. As this gets comfortable, raise the surface (higher bench, stairs) to get more vertical over weeks. |
| Downward dog hold | 3 × 20s    | Hands and feet on floor, push your hips up and back, press your chest toward your thighs. Straighten your legs as much as you can. Press your hands into the floor and try to push your shoulders open. Builds overhead stability and shoulder strength.    |

## Gym (Tuesday & Friday, 60 min)

Your exercise selection. Principles:

- Pull volume ≥ push volume
- Include rear delt and external rotation work every session
- Include forearm/wrist work every session
- Gym supports throwing, doesn't lead
